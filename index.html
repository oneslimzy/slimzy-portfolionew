About

The work does the talking.

I'm Slimzy — a graphic designer and motion editor running Slimzy Concepts out of Abuja, Nigeria. For the past few years I've helped tech, fintech and Web3 founders turn ideas into visuals people actually stop for: launch videos, promos, explainers, and the brand systems that hold them together.

I keep things punchy and minimal — on screen and off it. No filler, no fluff, just clean frames, sharp pacing and identity work built to hold up next to any global brand.
